Mango Spritzer

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces mango juice, cold
  • 2 ounces lime juice (about 2 limes)
  • 1 bottle ginger beer, cold
  • 2 lime wheels
  • 2 mango wedges (optional)
  • 2 sprigs of mint (optional)

Steps

  1. Divide the mango juice and lime juice between two glasses over ice, if desired. Top off with ginger beer.

  2. Add a lime wheel and a mango spear for garnish.
